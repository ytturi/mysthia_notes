The researcher in the woods
...

Known by a few in town, this researcher comes every now and then to get their wounds healed and limbs reattached. Noblemen comission magic items and consumables from him, although his secluded cottage is far from town.

Velien is a merchant town, however not many posts will sell many magic items, and not many potions may be available at the local temple. If adventurer's inquiry for magic items or potions to reinforce their adventures, this is their best candidate.

The name of this researcher is Aiden Starseeker. He lives secluded in his cottage in the woods, rarely visited by wealthy people to adquire some of his creations. He comes to town once every week or two, sometimes with a heavy wound or burn to be healed by the local clerics, but he always adquires almost a month worth of rations and some alchemical components.

In the last two weeks he hasn't passed by town, which worries some of the villagers. That's why they decide to inform the adventurers, so that they may wander and maybe provide some help.

Visiting the cabin
...

Aiden is an eccentric individual, however, he values the income provided by his little store as that boosts his research capabilities. In order to keep those simple-minded from visiting and only attract really interested customers he has a curious map.

Aiden Starseeker token

After dealing with the undeads from Emyr, the wild beast of Iemin and performing the healing ritual, the route is safe. Otherwise, consider adding a forest encounter according to the missing quest (Undeads, fiends or animals respectively).

Approaching the cabin
...

The cabin is actually a two-story tall building on a hill. It clearly has a basement as there are some windows on the ground, although the walls seem to be made out of rock and grass. Starting from the first story, the walls are made of stone and it has a tiled roof. Emerging from it is a small cillindrical tower about twenty feet in diameter.

A small ramp brings the trail to the main door which is located between two statues.

Walls and bushes prevent anyone from easily accessing the sidedoor. However, someone with enough iterest could climb the hillside to find a small cock enclosure and a small garden with some exotic flowers and some common vegetables (see 4: Garden).

The windows
...

The windows are small and they only open a bit. Only by breaking them, a small or smaller creature would be able to get in. If peeked through, the accessible windows close to the entrance goes towards a small studio, a table and some bookstands are visible from outside.

The other window on the northern side gives sight of a small room with a lot of crates and a big vault door.

The main door
...

The main door is locked and leads directly to the shop (see 1: Shop). Upon knocking on the door, the eyes of the statues will move looking at whomever knocked. A clear deep voice will then state:

"Aiden is not available, turn around and come another day. You can leave a message and he will tend to it as soon as possible. Venture into the house and the house will deal with you."

This is a small surveliance system that Aiden has, with which he can see whoever tries to get into the house from the inner room in the basement.

The message is a prerecorded magic mouth message, and knocking on the door, triggers an alert spell so that he is aware that there was visit.

Shortly after the message is spoken, another voice, cracked and old will speak through the statues mouth. This will be Aiden who is secluded in the saferoom.

Excuse excuse, ahh don't go! You seem capable! I'm afraid I can't attend customers but I could use some help. You see I was experimenting with something and... well let's say a small accident happened and it turned against me! If you are visiting I am sure I can repay you in one way or another!

Aiden will then explain the situation. He has some animated objects in the house that he can't disable as they are the security system for when he leaves. However, they should be easy enough to deal with if the adventurers are to deal with what is beneath the house.

He was researching how to create his own servant, a magic assistant of sorts, however, after the ritual was complete and he was enchanting their purpose, he wasn't careful enough with the wording and the creation turned against him.

After each sentence, the being repeats and the creator acknowledged. His orders were: "You will always protect the house, the creations and me from any intruder. Anyone who isn't me is an intruder." at which point it started attacking Aiden.

Aiden is in a safe room that the characters shouldn't reach. However he also warns the characters, the inner vault is not to be intruded.

Aiden then proceeds to inform of the situation:

His creation, a Galvan, is secluded in his workshop as he ran away and closed the vault door. This is the only known entry for his creation, with that one sealed it can't get away.

Aiden will continue:

Reach the basement, then I will open a path towards the workshop and seal it behind you until the threat is dealt with. Go back to the entrance and find me, I'll open the passage again and fix the Galvan. And please, don't steal from me, I will know it!

Ground Floor
...

1.1: Shop
...

An athletics (strength) DC 16 is required to break open the door. Alternatively, lockpicking with a successful (dexterity) DC 14 will open the door.

This room has several items in displays. From books and potions to a whole set of armor. Upon a closer look, most of the items are more curiosities than something useful. For instance there are some cubes that react to being touched and make some kind of a puzzle meant to entertain more than serve a greater purpose. However, there's a set of potions that is easily identified with a small piece of paper attached to it. There are:

  • Potion of Advantage
  • Potion of Healing
  • Potion of Greater Healing
  • Potion of Acid resistance
  • Potion of Fire resistance

After spending 10min searching around, a successful investigation (intelligence) DC 12 reveals some interesting artifacts:

  • Spell scroll: Tasha's Caustic Brew
  • Spell scroll: Spider climb
  • Spell scroll: Pyrotechnics
  • Spell scroll: Web
  • Spell scroll: Grease
  • Magic ink and paper used for spellbooks
  • Alchemical components worth 200gp

Behind the desk, and easy to find, there are some drawers, the first one contains a decent amount of coins in small pouches with 50 coins on each pouch. There's a total of 200pp, 700gp, 500sp, and 300cp.

The set of armor comes to live and attacks any trespassers when someone enters the door without Aiden saying "come in".

1.2: Living Room
...

Whomever enters the room without Aiden saying "come in" is attacked by the animated rustic chairs and table in this living room.

Aside from that, this large room gives to a small kitchen workshop with several ingidients, pans and pots. A clean fireplace is present although it does present signs of having been used in the past. A small set of kitchenware and bottles can be found as well.

On the northern side there is a door that leads to the corridor. On the southern side, a small storage space is close to the door to the garden and a small door leads to the toilet.

1.3: Corridor
...

This small corridor comes from the living room and goes to the staircase.

It is protected by two animated sets of armor. The same as with the living room and the shop, unless Aiden is present and says "come in", they will attack any trespasser.

At the end of the corridor, a set of circular staircases go both up towards the reading room and down towards the storage room.

1.4: Garden
...

The garden is bigger than one would expect from the front of the house.

There is a small enclosure with some cocks, with a small cover where they keep their nests. A small fountain close to the chimney seems to be continuously pouring water.

A tree gives quite some shadow and reaches all the way to the roof and beyond.

Finally, a small vegatable patch accompanied with some exotic vegetables is located beneath some elvish crystalware almost covered by grapevines.
Close to them, a door leads towards the living room. The door is not locked.

First floor
...

2.1 The reading room
...

This small corridor between the stairs and the sleeping room has several bookshelves and a pair of confortable furniture, suited for casual reading.

The stairs lead upwards towards the teleportation circle, and downwards towards the corridor.

At the end of the corridor, close to the bedroom entrance lies another animated armor. The same as before, unless Aiden speaks "come in" the set of armor will attack any trespasser.

2.2 The sleeping room
...

This luxorious sleeping room contains a small desk with several scrolls and notes as well as a bookshelf. These are the accounting logs (using numbers, expected delivery date, delivered or not, payed or not, and which item) and personal research notes that Aiden keeps close. Searching among them for 10min and a successful intelligence (investigation) DC 14 reveals some spell scrolls:

  • Spell scroll: Blink
  • Spell scroll: Leomund's Tiny Hut
  • Spell scroll: Phantom steed
  • Spell scroll: Passwall

A chest and a drawer contain mainly commoner clothes with the exception of a set of fancy clothes.

A bigger locked chest is located right beneath the bed. It is protected by a knock spell. A successful Strength (athletics) DC 22 or Dexterity (lockpick) DC 20 are needed in order to open the simple yet protected lock. The chest contains several sets of fancy clothes, documentation and customer information required to map which item was sold to whom and where to locate said customer. It also contains several items of each customer, potentially used for scrying in case of an unpaid service.

On the other side of the room, a small tailor's space is used by Aiden to create magic items based on clothing.
Close to it, behind some curtains is a toilet and a bathtub.
