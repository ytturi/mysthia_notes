Getting into the Argent Highs
...

It is with hardship that the adventurers can get into the Argent Highs, a neighborhood restricted only to those with permission, which usually means a lot of money or receiving an invitation from the local nobility.

There are some ways to achieve this like a blatant victory or tense combat in the main entertainment of the city, the local arena: Aegis Crux.

The local guilds may be able to schedule an appointment with the local nobility in exchange for some requests they may have.

The guards Veteran are pretty used to people wanting to get into the neighborhood and won't be persuaded into it, although they would let anyone that can pay the entry of 500gp.

Into the sewers
...

With the help of Elwin, the adventurers may walk into the shady neighborhood of the Riverdocks. An amalgam of shady dealers of dubious sources, chaotic buildings, and passages built on top of the great canal.

There are just a few walkable entrances to the upper sewers, and most of them are controlled by the local gangs, especially the Green Tongue bandits. They take a liking to the entrance in order to either reach wealthy customers on the other side or charge a tax on whoever ventures into the rich side to steal.

Sewers path

After getting into the sewers, only one door goes through, and after it, there are only dangers. Rumors of monstrosities, giant rats, and swarms of vermins are said to reside in these sewers, with but a few more monsters feeding on whoever ventures them.

The group must succeed in a Dexterity (stealth) DC 14 or face one of these monstrosities.

Conclusion

After the encounter, the party reaches a set of rooms that lead to a ladder. On the upper side of it, a grate "welcomes" them to the Argent Highs, where they are not completely safe, but unless suspicious, they should be able to walk freely.
