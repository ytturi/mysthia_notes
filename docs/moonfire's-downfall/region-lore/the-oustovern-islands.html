The dragon isles

The Oustovern Islands
...

The oustovern islands, also known as the dark islands or the dragon islands, is a harsh land where big draconic creatures roam in the wild, from drakes and wyverns to terrible dragons.

Storms usually hit these lands which gives most of the coasts a jagged cliff overview.

Oustovern is the old name for the region which means the land of Storms and Dragons. Storms are more usual than sun and the land seems to be covered by a dark veil, the ebony trees have dark green leaves and the rocky mountains were formed by volcano eruptions, which formed big piles of volcanic rocks.

Ruling
...

The islands are ruled by the Faedra family. Descendants of Shadow, Wild, Sea and Avaril elves natives to the land.

They are absolute rulers, however the next ruler is decided by merits and not by blood, appointed by a council of wise and capable people.

Sea based
...

Their closeness to the sea and the almost perpetual storms have shaped these islands into jagged coastlines and harsh lands.

However, those who grow accustomed, they find a great life at the shores as sea riders, fishermans and sailors. They are known for excelling on their shipyard skills and their sailing mastery across the seas.

Draconic nature
...

The island's innhabitants are mainly draconic creatures, from drakes to salamanders and even wyverns or mighty dragons.

One of the main products are the reagents gathered from such creatures.

The Oustovern islands are also known for their draconic riding skills and their draconic-riding based airforce.

The islands
...

This small set of islands is formed by a big island, commonly called as "the mainland" and formaly refered to as "Phoenix"; And two smaller islands, commonly called "northernlands" and "southernlands" refering to the position with the mainland as a reference and formaly refered to as "Dragonsbreath" and "Falconclaw".

The three islands are a formation of volcanic nature, with (at least) one inactive volcano on each island as the main landmark.

The northernlands are quite savage with only but one bastion safe from the dangerous foes in the land, the city of Anderaan, an old fortress and the last city on this side of the spine. The city is home of monster hunters and has quite a remarkable protection from its environment.

The southernlands are dangerous but not in the same sense as the northernlands. A wild jungle covers most of the land on an eternal conflict between the emerald conclave, a faction mainly formed by druids that tries to protect the land, and green dragons.

The mainlands are also a harsh place, however the landscape offers natural shelter to some of the aggressive creatures that inhabit these lands. The mainlands have several cities, each ruled by an elven bloodline.

Important landmarks
...

Within Dragonsbreath you can find the city of Anderaan. The last bastion of humanity on this side of the spine. The big volcano at the center is called "Yrthargungdr" which is the name of the red dragon that used to live there. Upon its defeat, the volcano became dull and eventually inactive.

Within Phoenix, one can find the cities of Alduriel at the center, Laudnaus at the northwest, Gaelira at the southwest, Naarwaard at the south or Farduun at the east.

Alduriel is the biggest city of these realms, being home of the High Tide University, where the Umbral wing, a school of wizardry resides. Alduriel is also the home of the Faedra family, the rulers of these lands.

Other landmarks within Phoenix involve: The sawlands on the west coastline and the jagged wingspan on the east coastline. Close to Alduriel, you can find the Tyruun, also known as the lake of tears. The petrified forest of Greywind covers a great piece of land on the west while the Darkstone peaks make room on the far northeast.

Whithin Falconclaw, you can find "Jormandr", the big volcano that is covered in snow almost the whole year. It is surrounded by the Hellaans jungle, commonly known as the Hell lands due to the word resemblance and the harsh creatures that inhabit it, although its literal translation means "vast vegetation that crawls the surface". Only the westside of the southernlands is inhabited and the main population is gathered around Karlaan, a city on the western coast.
